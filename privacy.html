Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 14, 2025

Tasterly is a personal food tracking app. Your data stays on your device. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data.

Who we are

Tasterly is published by an individual developer. For any questions, contact support@tasterly.app.

Information we collect

  • Content you add locally on your device, such as item names, ratings, photos, and notes
  • Diagnostics automatically collected by Apple (such as crash logs), if you have sharing enabled in iOS settings

App permissions

  • Camera and Photo Library access, only when you choose to scan barcodes or attach photos to items

No other device permissions are required to use Tasterly.

How we use information

  • All item data and photos you add stay on your device
  • Diagnostic information, if shared with Apple, may be used to improve app performance and fix issues

Sharing

Tasterly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data. No third-party SDKs or advertising libraries are used in this app.

Data retention and deletion

All your data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app will delete all associated data. You may also remove items manually at any time.

Children

Tasterly is not directed to children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child in error, please contact us.

Your rights

If you are located in California, the EEA, or the UK, you may have additional rights such as access, correction, or deletion of your data. Since Tasterly stores data only on your device, you can exercise these rights by deleting or editing items directly in the app, or by deleting the app entirely. For questions, email support@tasterly.app.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. If significant changes are made, the effective date at the top will be updated.
